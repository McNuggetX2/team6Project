Our Story

Founded in 2024, we set out to create hassle-free, user-friendly, and no-frills study platform that will help anyone create and organize their study materials and/or notes within an easy to use service.

This is an ongoing project, so you can rest assured that we will keep the service updated and optimized to best serve you!
